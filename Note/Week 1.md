## Part II Bioinfo Introduction
- **Question/Hypothesis-Driven Science**
  1. Qusetion OR Hypothesis: Based on Biological/Medical Knowledge, the thoughts for a interesting phenomenon
  2. Information: Biological/Medical Data such as Images OR Sequences
  3. Analysis: Deepseek the Information/Data. Clean the data and extract the Feature. Lots of analysis technology work here.
  4. Modeling: Found a model to match the phenomenon and data. Including Probabilistic Model & Computational Algorithm
